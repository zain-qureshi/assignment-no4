About Us

Our School is one of Best Secondary Schools.

We focus on scholastic development, personal growth, and ethical enrichment. The purpose of Lexicon Group Of Schools is to prepare students with the promise of enhancing their intellectual, physical, social, emotional, spiritual, and artistic growth so that they may realize their power for good as citizens of Pakistan.

School aims at providing students with a well-rounded education offering: A caring Environment with strong Education foundation. An exclusive focus on the individual child.

Our Value We Promote a positive energetic and caring atmosphere in which children feel safe and secure and want to come to school. We are dedicated to equality and opportunity where all students are respected. Knowledge, Skills, Attitude and Values are developed to prepare the children for their next stage of learning and enable them to be successful in their community. We expert everyone to develop and show a sense of responsibility and self-discipline.
